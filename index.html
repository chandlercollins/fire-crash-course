View 4 — The Setup

Set it once, then stay carefree.

How the $120k actually happens. The big rocks auto-deduct inside each payroll system; net pay lands in checking; then automatic transfers fund the rest. The magic: once it's wired up, the plan runs itself — you just keep an eye on rent.

Foundation first: before any of this, always keep 3–6 months of expenses in a high-yield savings account — a safety net, so you never have to sell investments in a pinch.
